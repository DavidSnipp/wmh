Weddings at Worplesdon

If you are thinking of tying the knot then think about being married here at Worplesdon. Worplesdon village offers a wonderful experience. A traditional church wedding followed by a village reception.

St. Mary’s Church, Worplesdon, offers a traditional wedding service in an historic setting.

From the Lych Gate, a footpath winds up to the church porch

The church interior retains its original feel, hardly changed from earlier times

Enjoy being married in the traditional way. Weddings have been conducted at St. Mary’s for many years

After the ceremony, enjoy a time with your photographer outside this historic village church.

Then move to the Memorial Hall in the village to continue your festivities.
